Sustainability Practices

Sustainability is a core value at Petrol Terminal Logistics. We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact through responsible and sustainable practices. Our sustainability initiatives include:

Emission Reduction: Utilizing vapor recovery systems and other technologies to significantly reduce emissions from our storage operations.

Energy Efficiency: Implementing energy-efficient systems and practices to reduce our overall energy consumption.

Water Conservation: Adopting water-saving measures and recycling initiatives to minimize water usage.

Waste Management: Ensuring proper disposal and recycling of waste materials to minimize our ecological footprint.

SHIP MANAGEMENT

Offering First-Class Ship Management services to third parties.

STM Shipmanagement (STM) is a leading Geneva-based third-party ship management company focusing on a diversified fleet of tanker and dry vessels as well as offshore support vessels.

STM has a very large proven track record with more than 100 ships which have been under its management since inception, ranging from small tankers to Suezmax, containers, and dry units for a multitude of Owners and disponent Owners.

STM has not only safely carried million tons of wet, dry bulk, and thousands of containers, but also safely executed more than 20'000 voyages, dealt with more than 10'000 demurrage files, completed more than 40'000 port calls, and conducted more than 10'000 bunker operations with all major suppliers.
